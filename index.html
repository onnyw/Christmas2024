Merry Christmas 2024


 

 

Time seemed to be going faster and faster as we grew older! 2024 had been a difficult year because of the repid rise in the cost of living as well as the wars in many parts of the world! Thanksfully, Australia remained fairly peaceful and we thank God for keeping our family safe and provided!


On continued to work part-time for C&MA and he also volunteered as a religious Instructor once a week in a local primary school. In Nov, he led a team of young people to Papua, Indonesia for a short-term mission trip. It was a memorable experience to work alongside the C&MA missionaries and to serve the local young people in Indonesia.

In Mar, On and Brannie went to Europe for a 18 days holiday. We joined a tour and visited many cities, including Prague, Bratisalva, Vienna, Budapest, Ljubjana, Vience, Palermo, Palma, Barcelona, Marseille, Genoa and Rome. It was wonderful to see so many beautiful cities. The sceneries were beautiful and the weather was good most of the time. The highlight of the trip was the night when we walked around the Colosseum in Rome.


Brannie is now working only part-time as a relief teacher. She also worked a couple of days every week as a volunteer in RSPCA and in a local hospital. She enjoyed spending more time at home and she treasured the many hours of therapeutic sessions with her cats. In Mar, Brannie went back to Hong Kong to visit family and in Dec, Brannie went to Thailand for a short-term mission trip.

Andre and Ryan also took some times off from their full-time works for their overseas holidays. Andre went to New Zealand in Jun and Ryan went to Japan in Nov.

We thank God for keeping us safe and healthy!
We pray that you will find peace and love during this Christmas Season!

Wish you all a Blessed Christmas
and a Joyful New Year!

On, Brannie, Andre & Ryan
